Description

This recipe is an easy 5-ingredient recipe for chocolate chip cookies. They are very easy to make and delicious!

Ingredients

    3 Cups All Purpose Flower
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup butter softened
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

Steps

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). 2. Sift flour and brown sugar into a bowl; stir in eggs. Mix butter into flour mixture until dough is creamy; fold in chocolate chips. Spoon dough onto a baking sheet, keeping them about 3 inches apart. 3. Bake in the preheated oven until slightly brown, 10 to 15 minutes. Cool
